UNIVERSITY-UiTM LENDU,MELAKA.(PRA DIPLOMA)

I studied at this school in 2019-2020. (6 months)

UiTM Alor Gajah Campus is one of the branches of Universiti Teknologi MARA located in the state of Melaka , Malaysia . The campus was established in August 1984 in Bachang. In January 1994 , the campus moved to its permanent location in Lendu, about 5 kilometers from the town of Alor Gajah.. On 2 May 1996, the UiTM Melaka Campus (then ITM Melaka) was inaugurated by the YAB Prime Minister of Malaysia, Dato 'Seri Mahathir Mohamad. As of March 2020, Alor Gajah Campus students have surpassed 6,500 people while 255 support staff and 319 lecturers. Some of these lecturers teach on all three campuses.

There are 8 Faculties and 13 programs offered at UiTM Alor Gajah Campus, namely in Diploma 13 programs and Degree 3 programs. The faculties are the Faculty of Business Management, Faculty of Communication and Media Studies, Faculty of Accounting, Faculty of Hotel and Tourism Management, Faculty of Art and Design, Academy of Language Studies, Academy of Islamic and Contemporary Studies, and the Department of Law.
